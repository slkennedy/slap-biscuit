Slap Biscuit

A game for the corporate masses.

Two players face off in the ultimate test of strategic ablity. Alternating turns, players place the biscuit on their designated starting corner of the conference table. They then take aim and attempt to slap the biscuit into the basket while their opponent tries to defend the basket.
